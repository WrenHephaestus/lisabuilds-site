# Tweet 022 -- Context Window Truth

Your Claude context window degrades at 70-75% capacity. Not at 100%.

Research measured it: quality drops around 147K of 200K tokens. The last 25-30% looks normal from outside but precision is already compromised.

This is why short sessions with strong CLAUDE.md files beat long sessions every time.

Your context file reloads at full fidelity. Your conversation doesn't.
