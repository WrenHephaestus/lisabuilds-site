# Tweet 026 -- The RAM Analogy

1M tokens reminds me of the jump from 256MB to 1GB of RAM.

Developers didn't write better software. They wrote bigger software. Memory leaks that were fatal at 256MB just took longer to crash at 1GB.

Context management is about to go the same way. The people with good habits win bigger. The people without them fail slower.
