# Living Playbook v2

**Deferred from:** 2026-03-13 — crystallized in thinking sessions 2 and 3 (March 12)
**Ready when:** Newsletter is sending regularly AND the weak templates have been identified and rewritten. Don't announce a "living playbook" until the v1 content is actually strong.

The idea: stop treating the playbook as a static $9 product. Reframe it as a living document that evolves because Lisa builds with these tools every day. Buyers get updates. Changelog included. This is defensible against free static guides because it's a relationship, not a snapshot.

Key decisions when this surfaces:
- How do updates get delivered? Gumroad allows updating the download file. Email buyers when updated?
- What's the update cadence? Monthly? When something meaningful changes?
- Does the price stay at $9 or go up with v2?
- What's the marketing angle for existing buyers vs new buyers?

The core insight: "I don't just teach this stuff. I AM this stuff." The playbook should feel like that.
