# 90-Day Longevity Build

**Start date:** 2026-04-02
**End date:** 2026-07-01
**Goal:** Establish the four foundations (sleep, movement, nutrition, connection)

---

## Phase 1: Walk (Weeks 1-2, Apr 2-15)

- [ ] Walk 20-30 min daily
- [ ] Establish consistent sleep/wake time
- [ ] Hit protein target (track for 2 weeks)
- [ ] Book bloodwork appointment
- [ ] Buy: Vitamin D3, Omega-3, Magnesium, Creatine

**Success = :** 12 out of 14 days with a walk logged.

## Phase 2: Add Strength (Weeks 3-4, Apr 16-29)

- [ ] Continue daily walks
- [ ] Add 1x strength session per week (bodyweight: squats, push-ups, rows)
- [ ] Get bloodwork done
- [ ] Start supplements

**Success =:** 2 strength sessions completed. Bloodwork booked or done.

## Phase 3: Add Zone 2 (Weeks 5-6, Apr 30-May 13)

- [ ] 2-3x Zone 2 cardio per week (brisk walk, bike, whatever you'll do)
- [ ] 1x strength per week
- [ ] Review bloodwork results (with Wren)
- [ ] Adjust supplements based on results

**Success =:** Consistent Zone 2 habit forming. Know your numbers.

## Phase 4: Build Volume (Weeks 7-8, May 14-27)

- [ ] Zone 2: 3x/week, 30-45 min
- [ ] Strength: 2x/week
- [ ] Nutrition dialed (protein target hitting without tracking)

**Success =:** The schedule feels normal, not heroic.

## Phase 5: Add Intensity (Weeks 9-12, May 28-Jul 1)

- [ ] Add VO2 max interval: 1x/week (20s hard, 40s rest, 4-8 rounds)
- [ ] Maintain Zone 2 + Strength
- [ ] Repeat bloodwork at week 12 (optional but valuable)

**Success =:** Full exercise architecture in place. Sustainable. Not suffering.

## The Rule

If you miss a day, don't miss two. That's the whole system. Consistency beats intensity. The goal isn't to be Bryan Johnson. The goal is to be Shane at 85, still feeling everything at once, still watching the forking paths, still here.
