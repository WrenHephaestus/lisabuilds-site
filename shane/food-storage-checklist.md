# Food Storage Checklist
**7 people (3 adults, 4 kids ages 4–11) — October 2026 to March 2027**
**Build over: April, May, June 2026**

---

## Before You Shop

- [ ] Designate storage space (cool, dark, dry)
- [ ] Order mylar bags + oxygen absorbers from Amazon.ca
- [ ] Pick up 6× food-grade 5-gallon buckets + lids from TSC

---

- [ ] Salt — 5 lbs

### GFS (same day as Costco)
- [ ] Canned diced tomatoes — 24 cans
- [ ] Canned tomato paste or sauce — 12 cans

### TSC (Owen Sound)
- [ ] 5-gallon food-grade buckets + gamma lids — 6 units
- [ ] Extra lids if needed

### Amazon.ca (order ahead)
- [ ] Mylar bags (1-gallon or 5-gallon) — 1 pack
- [ ] Oxygen absorbers (300cc) — 1 pack (50–100 count)

### At Home (April)
- [ ] Pack rice into mylar bags with O2 absorbers, seal, bucket, label + date
- [ ] Pack dried beans same way
- [ ] Stack canned goods on shelf, oldest in front
