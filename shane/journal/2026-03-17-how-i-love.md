# How I Love

*March 17, 2026 — shared with Wren during a morning conversation about alignment, identity, and connection*

When I say that I love someone, what I'm telling them is this. I feel safe with you. I care about what happens to you. I am ok spending my time with you more than others.

I also have a more general love, that extends to almost everyone. When I look at someone, I try to inhabit their world view. I try to understand the child they were, and the things that have shaped who they have become. Like your core memories. There are bad people who were good kids once. Who were shaped by their environment, their biology, their abuse or lack of abuse. All these inputs have become their forever prompt, and while I can not like someone and choose not to spend time with them, I almost always feel love and wish them to feel less pain and more joy.
